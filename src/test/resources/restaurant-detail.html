De nieuwe winkel

  • Gebroeders Van Limburgplein, Nijmegen, 6511 BW, Netherlands
  • 45 - 95 EUR • Organic, Vegetarian
MICHELIN Guide’s Point Of View

Emile van der Staak’s vegetable-based cuisine focuses mainly on herbs and plants – although fish and meat also feature, they play a secondary role. Dishes are full of creativity, with remarkable – and sometimes surprising – contrasts in flavour. Discover nature in all its guises in this spacious, soberly decorated restaurant.
